Description

I made a silly little forest. Despite the ordering this was the first of the 3 and its where I figured out how the coloring works. I wanted to add a source of interaction so in the forest campus with the greys to represent buildings and the browns to represent dirt, we have students you can place.

Technical

I used p5.js to create stick figures out of the original yellow dot. They represent students roaming the campus. I also made it more rare to see non-green colors since we're looking at UCSC from a more top down view.

Reflection

This one was a modified version of the professors but it helped as a starting point for minecraft and train.
